Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular Indian flatbread stuffed with a spiced potato filling. It is typically served with yogurt, pickles, or butter and is a common breakfast dish in North India.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • Ghee or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour with a pinch of salt. Gradually add water and knead to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, mix the boiled and mashed potatoes with chopped onions, green chilies, cilantro, cumin seeds, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Ensure the mixture is well combined.
  3. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Roll out one ball into a small circle on a floured surface.
  4. Place a spoonful of the potato filling in the center of the rolled-out dough. Bring the edges together to seal the filling inside and pinch off any excess dough.
  5. Gently flatten the stuffed dough ball and roll it out again into a larger circle, being careful not to let the filling spill out.
  6. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium heat. Place the rolled-out paratha on the hot tawa and cook for 1-2 minutes until you see bubbles forming on the surface.
  7. Flip the paratha and apply ghee or oil on the cooked side. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, then flip again and apply ghee or oil on the other side. Cook until both sides are golden brown and cooked through.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot with yogurt, pickles, or butter. Enjoy your delicious Aloo Paratha!
